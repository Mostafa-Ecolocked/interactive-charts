This research investigated the effects of graphene oxide (GO) and ground granulated blast furnace slag (GGBFS) on the properties of high-performance concrete (HPC). GO enhanced compressive and flexural strength. The combination of GO and GGBFS further improved mechanical properties and durability compared to using either alone. GO dosage was crucial, with an optimum range identified for the best performance.
